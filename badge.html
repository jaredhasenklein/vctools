Name Badge Generator

Instructions:

  1. Log in to VMS and select your event.
  2. Click All Reports.
  3. Click Badges.
  4. Click Export.
  5. Click the button below to select your export.
  6. Select your export type from the list (either Avery 5163 for a sheet of 10 labels or Dymo 2x4 for use with thermal printers).
  7. Optionally, enter text into one or more of the custom text fields (event name works great in the top slot!).
  8. The PDF will automatically download and a preview will render below.
All data processing happens on your computer and is never sent to a server. No worries about PII here! Just remember that the file you download from here may contain PII, just like the export from VMS.
